{{#i18n}}How to connect your PayPal account{{/i18n}}
{{#i18n}}In addition to entering your PayPal email address in the form below, you must configure your PayPal Business Standard/Advanced or Pro Account to allow Smart Store eCommerce transactions to be processed using your PayPal account. You will need to do this even if you are already connected to other third party eCommerce providers.{{/i18n}}
{{#i18n}}Click here to create your PayPal Business Standard/Advanced or Pro Account{{/i18n}}, {{#i18n}}or if you already have a PayPal Business Standard/Advanced or Pro Account{{/i18n}} {{#i18n}}download instructions about how to configure your PayPal account{{/i18n}} {{#i18n}}for your Cinsay Smart Store. For additional assistance, email us at{{/i18n}} support@cinsay.com.

{{#i18n}}Important Notes and restrictions{{/i18n}}

{{#i18n}}PayPal does not allow merchants to offer guest checkout (checkout without a PayPal account) unless your PayPal Business Standard/Advanced or Pro account is verified by PayPal.{{/i18n}} {{#i18n}}Please click here to review the PayPal FAQs for verifying your PayPal Business Standard/Advanced or Pro account with PayPal{{/i18n}}.
